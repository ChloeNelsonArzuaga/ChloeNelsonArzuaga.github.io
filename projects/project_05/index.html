Electronic Design of a Hello World Board

This week we were tasked with recreating a hello world board and adding an LED and Button to it. This week was particularly challenging for me becasue I had never done electronics design before and my only electronics manufacturng experience was from a couple weeks ago in this class. I also spent a bit of time working on my how to make something big for next week so I did not have as much time to work on this weeks project.

I started this weeks project by reading over tutorials and watching plenty of videos on how to use Eagle, the software needed to make the circuit board. Once I felt slightly comfortable with the software (it was still totally confusing), I began to insert my components. I used Neil's image above as a reference.

One thing that took me a while to realize was that I needed to download the fab library (fab.lb) that had all the components I needed. Prior to that I was trying to find them all in the already downloaded libaries. Once I figured that out it was a lot easier to find all of the components and place them in the scematic.

Once I had all of the components in the scematic, I started to connect them with wires. One thing that I found very annoying was the fact that the micro-controllers pins were not in the same layout on the scematic so I had a lot of wires crossing. I am a bit of a perfectionist and so I wanted to get this part as perfect as I could so it took quite a while.

After all of my work placing things perfectly in the scematic, I was a little sad when I clicked convert ot board and all of my components stacked on top of eachother. It took a lot of time to get them back where I originally wanted them to go.

Finally I got them all back to where I wanted them and drew in the wires becasue all of the yellow lines were just straight shots from one component to another which ment that a lot of them were crossed over. Unfortunatly I did not have enought time to actually make the board becasue I ran into a lot of problems on the design side but will come back an finish it at a later time.
